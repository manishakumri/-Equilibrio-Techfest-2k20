PET BOTTLE ROCKET

Event of Alchemist

PET BOTTLE ROCKET is an event involving basic engineering knowledge and hence students of all branches can participate. It is an interesting event. The rocket needs to have a simple design, which would be easy to construct and the apparatus needed are inexpensive .The purpose of organizing this event is to provide information related to TIME OF FLIGHT and RANGE achieved by model of rocket. These useful information are used for dimensional analysis of prototype.

Details

  • EVENT FORMAT : THERE WILL BE TWO ROUNDS OF THE EVENT

    • Rules

    Event of managerial

      Round 1

    • First round is qualifying round. In this round, distance is the qualifying criteria. The minimum distance rocket should travel is 50m. Single point is given for every meter. 30% weightage for 1st round.

      • ROUND 2

    • Second round is the final round and this round is focused on TIME OF FLIGHT.

      • In this how much time the rocket remains in the sky is measured. For this, team has to design a parachute for their rocket and it should open while the rocket is in the sky. Five points are awarded for each second. 70% weightage for 2nd round. (Maximum two participant are allowed in a team)

    Prizes

    • Top 2 winners will be awarded with prizes and merit certificate.
    • 3rd will be awarded with certificate only
