About The Event

About Us

Equilibrio, the annual techno management fest of Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur.Since it's inception in 2013, Equilibrio has emerged as a prime curator of creative minds.Initiated with an aim to serve a prefect launchpad for technocrats to showcase their technical skills and inventiveness. It also encompasses a platform for various activities which includes competitive events, motivating guest talks, workshops, cultural programs and exhibitions. Being a festival, Equilibrio not only celebrates technology but also promotes a social cause every year. Every year a social cause is considered and students add towards it's rectification in their surroundings and this year will be no different. After its debut, the event has been growing in calibre and popularity. EQUILIBRIO 2k20 will kick off from 16th January and the glory will continue for two days. Dive into this extravaganza and experience a complete package of fun, thrill and excellence.

New IT Building, Guru Ghasidas Central University

FROM THE DESK OF VC

Nurturing creativity and inspiring innovation are two of the key elements of a successful student, and Equilibrio is the perfect amalgamation of both. The annual technical festival of School of Studies Engg. And Technology provides a rightful contingency to nurture technical skills and impart fine qualities to the students. With the aim of continuing this feat of producing think tanks from our country, Equilibrio supports the development of such minds who can challenge real-life problems and develop solutions for them. Year after year, students pushing their limits and striving for more which has been possible through the diligent dedication and hard work between students and teachers. Equilibrio extends an opportunity to take up initiatives that not only create awareness among people but also provide engineering solutions to those social agonies. The events and competitions from every possible branch of science and management, challenging some of the best minds to prove their technical expertise and competitive spirit. I express my considerable appreciation and best wishes to all the students and participants. I hope this year Equilibrio will make it Bigger and Better, than ever!




Prof. ANJILA GUPTA
