job battle

Event of managerial

JOB Battle is an event based on the real life job situations in which the candidate has to undergo several rounds such as aptitude test, group discussion, personal interview .The event tries to create a replica of the real job situations.

Details

Questions will be based on CAT exam pattern.

  • EVENT FORMAT : THERE WILL BE TWO ROUNDS OF THE EVENT

    • Rules

    Event of managerial

      Round 1 : written round

    • The paper is split between three sections namely Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC), Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DI-LR) and Quantitative Ability (QA).
    • Each section contains 15 questions.
    • Time span-1hour.
    • Marking scheme:- correct response (+4),wrong response(-1)
    • Questions are in both MCQ and non-MCQ format.

      • ROUND 2 : group discussion

    • Groups will be formed of 4 students.
    • Topics will be provided on the basis of current issues, any social topic.
    • 2 min time will be given to think over the topic.
    • 5 min time will be given in which you have to put your points.
    • Judging criteria will be speaking skill, confidence, topic presentation.
    • OUTPUT PROVIDED SHOULD NOT INVOLVE MAJOR CHANGE IN THE BODY

      • ROUND 3 : personal interview

    • It will be like like any other interview topic will be decided on the spots and rules will be decided by the interviewers

    Prizes

    • 1st Place - INR 3000/-
    • 2nd Place - INR 2000/-
