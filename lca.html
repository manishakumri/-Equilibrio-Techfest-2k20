LIGHT CAMERA ACTION

In Lights, Camera, Action! We’ll encourage desire participants who had made short films or want to make short films to present their message via short films, the best short film will be rewarded with price money and certificate.

Details

DURATION :


▪For micro film- under 6 min
▪For short film- under 12 min Including credits- 15 min (exceptional)

EVENT FORMAT :


Must be shot in MPEG4/AVI.
▪The recommended ratios are 16:9/16:9 Full Height Anamorphic- in DV/HDV.

Marking Scheme::


▪Video Quality
▪Sound Quality
▪Acting
▪Direction
▪Cinematography
▪Back Ground Score ▪Length and effectiveness of the story

Rules

Event of Cultural

    Round 1 :


    ▪Must be shot after 1st January 2k18.
    ▪It can be shot in any language but subtitled in English.
    ▪Entries must be submitted online.
    ▪Website link will be mailed to team leader's e-mail ID.
    ▪Movie can be of any genre.
    ▪It can be live-action, animation or combo of both.
    ▪No adult content.
    ▪No copyright content.
    ▪Signed documents saying it's their own content.
    ▪Responsibility and Disclaimer document.
    ▪Length is strict.
    ▪Details of the process should be mentioned(equipment n locations used including shooting date).
    ▪Crew details.
    ▪Film should be uploaded in our prescribed place.

Prizes

  • 1st Place - INR 2500/-
