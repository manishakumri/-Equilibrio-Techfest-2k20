    Event of AAKAAR

      RULES

    • One team can have a maximum of 2 members.
    • The event consists of two rounds of participation.
    • The 1st round is slip test. Qualifiers of first round will advance to the next round. The 2nd round is building structure.
    • Participants have to construct a structure with the provided material.
    • Participants have to construct the structure in such a way so that the load can be placed on it.
    • Maximum time provided is 30 minutes.
    • The structure should have dimensions: Minimum height-35cm.

      • JUDGING CRITERIA

    • The load is placed on the structure. After the structure is placed on the vibrator.
    • Marking will be done on the basis of the duration the structure can hold itself. The structure which can withstand for longer duration will be the winner.
