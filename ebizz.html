WHY STARTUP?


NOW-A-DAYS EVERYONE IS FED UP WITH THEIR JOBS AND WANTS TO BEGIN A STARTUP AND GET THEIR JOB SATISFACTION. WE THROUGH THIS EVENT, WANT TO PROVIDE A PLATFORM TO CURVE YOUR PATH TOWARDS STARTUP WORLD.     Round 1:challenger/qualifier

  • THIS IS AN ONLINE ROUND.
  • THERE WILL BE 50 QUESTIONS BASED ON MARKETING,MATHEMATICAL APTITUDE AND GENERAL APTITUDE.
